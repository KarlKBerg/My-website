My Front-end journey

25.Sep.2025

by Karl Berg

Welcome to my website! I am currently studying front-end development at Noroff. Here, I share my road to become a front-end developer. I document my learning process, projects, and experiences as we go through them. I will use this mainly for my own journey, but also for others that might find it interesting. Feel free to explore and follow along!
